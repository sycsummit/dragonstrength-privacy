Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 18, 2025  ·  DragonStrength for iOS

DragonStrength is built on a simple principle: your workout data belongs to you, not us. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles — and what it doesn't.

01 Data We Do Not Collect

DragonStrength does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. We have no servers. There are no user accounts, no logins, no emails, and no analytics pipelines.

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting sent to us
  • No third-party SDKs, trackers, or advertising frameworks

02 Data Stored on Your Device

All of your data lives locally on your device. This includes:

  • Exercise database — your customized list of exercises, stored as JSON files in the app's Documents directory
  • Workout logs — completed workout records stored via SwiftData (Apple's local persistence framework)
  • App preferences — settings such as training mode, weight increments, and active profile, stored in UserDefaults

This data never leaves your device except through iCloud sync (see below) or an explicit export action you initiate.

03 iCloud Sync

DragonStrength uses Apple's CloudKit framework, which may sync your workout and exercise data across your personal iCloud-connected devices.

This data is yours, synced through your own iCloud account. It is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We cannot access, view, or retrieve your iCloud data.

iCloud sync can be managed or disabled through your device's Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

04 Data Export

DragonStrength includes an Export feature that allows you to save a copy of your data as a JSON file. This file is generated entirely on your device. You control where it goes — we never receive a copy.

05 Children's Privacy

DragonStrength does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app complies with COPPA and similar regulations by design.

06 Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update the effective date above and note the change in the App Store release notes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
