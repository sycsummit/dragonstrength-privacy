The best way to report an issue or suggest an improvement is to send an email with as much detail as possible:

02 Frequently Asked Questions

My workout data disappeared after updating the app.
Your data is stored locally on your device and should persist across updates. If you have iCloud enabled, check that DragonStrength has iCloud access in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

How do I back up my data?
Go to the Profile Manager and tap Export All Data. This saves a JSON file containing all your exercises and workout history that you can store anywhere you like.

Can I use DragonStrength on multiple devices?
Yes — if you're signed into the same iCloud account on both devices, your data will sync automatically via CloudKit.

How do I delete all my data?
Delete the app from your device. All locally stored data will be removed. To also remove iCloud data, go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage and remove DragonStrength.

03 Privacy

DragonStrength does not collect any personal data. Your workout information stays on your device and your iCloud account. Read our full Privacy Policy.
